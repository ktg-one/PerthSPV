Mission Brief

Mission Parameters

This strategic blueprint outlines the execution protocol for a high-yield industrial property roll-up in Perth. The objective is to acquire "boring" logistics assets under a strict $2M SPV cap while servicing 12%+ private capital costs.

Execution

Capital Recycling Protocol

The 18-month execution cycle extracts private capital through refinancing, recycling it into the next SPV.

01

Month 0-3: Acquisition & Value Add

Purchase under-rented asset. Bridge finance deployment.

Key Actions:
  • Secure Bank Debt (60%) + Private Bridge (40% @ 12.5%).
  • Execute immediate cosmetic Capex (Painting, Signage).
  • Initiate rent reviews to market levels.
